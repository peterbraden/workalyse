Working better with Workalyse

Companies are very good at optimizing for their customers. Techniques such as AB testing give detailed information on how to improve a product based on data.

But a product is only half of a company. The people in that company are just as important.

We want to help you work better by applying the same techniques used to improve products to your job. We want to measure your job satisfaction and productivity, and empower you to improve your day-to-day life.

By allowing you to test simple experiments, and recording how productive and happy it made you, we hope that you will be able to see clear increases in your performance and happiness.

It's completely free to sign up and try - just give us your work email and we'll send you instructions on how to start.
